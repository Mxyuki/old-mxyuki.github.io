    Party Ranking

    What is a Party Ranking ? It's simple , you take an Anime or an Artist and you ask many people to rank the songs of this Artist/Anime , After that people Rank a added together to know which song was the most Loved , After that a video is made going from the Least Liked Music to the Most Liked Music.

    Anime Exchange

    What is an Anime Exchange ? It's simple , At the start of Each Month a Google Doc is sent , everyone that want to join just need to fill this doc , after few days you will receive a DM on Discord by the Organisator that will tell you to who you have to give an Anime , After you gave an Anime you will also receive an Anime that you must finish before the end of the month , At the end of the month you make a Review of the Anime.

    AMQ Scripts

    What are AMQ Scripts ? It's simple , It's code that you can Add to AMQ using Tampermonkey , Those script are made to make your Quality Of Life on AMQ better or just script that make something Fun, the script shared here are Scripts made by Miyuki.
